Explain a situation where you have had an ethical decision to make. Discuss how you weighed up the values involved in that decision, the decision you made, and the consequences of your action.

Early this year I had decided to change careers from accounting and move into software development, I had put through my initial application into Dev-Academy while still being employed. I had to decide whether to tell my current employer that I was seeking to change careers without any guarentee that I had been accepted into the course. I knew if I told my employer late, not only would it impact his ability to hire someone new in time but it would leave a number of client businesses at risk of not being supported by their accountant/virtual CFO, and tarnish my relationship with my manager and teammates. I decided to be open and tell my employer of my intentions and kept an open dialog that nothing was set in stone as I hadn't been accepted into the course. They were appreciative that I was open and honest, it gave them time to consider the team structure and have time to hire before my departure.
